Introduction to Painting Course

by Lynn Hollis

In this course led by Bath Spa art graduates it is a chance to get messy and create artwork with all the basics as well as some advanced brush techniques. It is open for all ages and the more the merrier!

Requirements

No requirements needed apart from we advise you not to wear any clothes that you may not want to get paint on, we will provide all equipment as well as aprons, but you never know where a stray flick of the brush can make paint end up.
